The Builder Model

Multi-skilled people who ship end-to-end — AI handles 70% of execution, builders handle judgment

Specialist Model (Before)
12 People

4-5 handoffs per campaign

Builder Model (After)
7 People

Brief in, output out
Bottleneck: judgment
Builders cross-cover
🛠
Builders Ship End-to-End
A builder takes a brief and delivers the finished output — no handoffs to a designer, writer, or developer. They direct AI agents for 70% of execution and apply human judgment for the remaining 30%.
π
Pi-Shaped Skills
Each builder is deep in 1-2 disciplines and competent across their pod's full scope. AI fills the gaps between breadth and depth. Miranda doesn't need to be a great designer — she needs to know when AI design output is good enough to ship.
⚙
Two Pods, Clean Ownership
Performance Pod runs the revenue engine. Internal Agency Pod runs brand, creative, and stakeholder service. Each pod is self-contained — the Marketing Ops Manager runs the agency, Paul runs performance. Stephen sets strategy across both.

Key Risks

Considerations

What needs to go right for the builder model to work

Unicorn Hire Risk
Performance Builder

Finding someone genuinely multi-skilled across 7 performance disciplines AND AI-native is hard. May need 6-8 weeks and a premium salary. Paul covers execution gaps during hiring.

Bus Factor
Performance Pod

One Performance Builder = single point of failure for the revenue engine. Mitigation: Paul can cover, AI agents retain workflows. Consider second builder long-term.

Builder vs Specialist Labels
Agency Pod

If Matthew only writes, Miranda only does social, and Alisha only designs — they are specialists with builder labels. Cross-training must be enforced over 3-4 months.

Builder Burnout
All Builders

Research shows 60% burnout in IT generalists. Broad scope + AI management is heavy cognitive load. Monitor actively, protect capacity. AI must reduce load, not just increase output expectations.

Quality Depth
Trade-off

Builders won't match specialists in any single discipline. The bet: AI + good-enough judgment > deep specialist + slow handoffs. May not hold for complex technical SEO or advanced attribution.

Marketing Ops Manager Hire
Critical Path

This person runs the internal agency. Agency background essential — knows how to run creative services, manage stakeholders, and operate AI-native. A bad hire here breaks the entire brand/creative side.
